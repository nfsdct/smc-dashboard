📋 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY — MARCH 2026
💰 Media Performance
March performance was mixed across campaigns, impacted by partial budgets, shorter flighting, and a strategic shift due to the geopolitical situation. Staycation was the standout performer, significantly exceeding all KPIs. Search campaigns delivered strong efficiency and CTR-driven quality despite reduced scale.
🌐 Website Performance
Traffic fell to 659.6K sessions (−96.1% MoM) as large-scale campaign activity wound down and regional conflict suppressed travel intent. Travel advisory page: 35% partner link click rate vs 12.7% average. Organic impressions (11.7M) exceeded 10.8M target.
🌟 Influencer Performance
81 UAE-based influencers (20 Emiratis) generated 50M+ impressions through staycation-led content. Eid activations including Hussein Al Jassmi and Ahmed Saad concerts delivered solid engagement. International travel halts limited full KPI delivery despite positive local momentum.
🏷️ Brand Health
TripAdvisor searches declined 82% YOY and 67% vs 2022. Q4 2025: awareness +0.2pp, familiarity +0.7pp, consideration +0.6pp, and intent +0.4pp. Kuwait leads on awareness and intent; KSA ranks highest on familiarity and consideration.
📰 PR Performance
Destination PR generated 2.6B+ reach and 3M+ EMV through Eid content, Chairman storytelling, and #InAbuDhabi integration. Corporate delivered 906M reach and AED 1.6M EMV across 329 clippings (+400% MoM reach).
📱 Social Performance
Destination reach (−65.2%), views (−54.3%) and engagement (−67.4%) declined due to reduced posting and regional context. ER underperformed (0.39% vs 0.99%). Corporate: 1.7M reach; Ramadan drove strong sentiment. Calendar: 701K reach.

✅ SUCCESSES
Staycation Pivot Drove Standout Campaign Performance
Strategic shift to staycation-led targeting exceeded all KPIs. Broader reach optimization unlocked strong impressions, engagement and traffic, turning a constraint into a commercial opportunity.
Local Influencer Advocacy Generated Authentic Destination Storytelling

🔁 CARRY FORWARD
Strengthening Social Consistency & Sentiment Recovery
Reduced posting volumes and geopolitical context drove engagement and positive sentiment below plan. Rebuilding consistent content cadence with culturally resonant storytelling will be critical heading into Q2.
Scaling International Influencer & Celebrity
Contracted celebrity deliverables and international influencer trips were halted mid-quarter. Future planning should build contingency windows and prioritize rescheduling high-impact programs.
